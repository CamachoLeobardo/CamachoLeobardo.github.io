Reseach


My main interest is boundary control and estimation of dynamic systems described by partial differential equations. My research focuses on state estimation for reaction-diffusion equations and the main application of the algorithms is state-of-charge and state-of-health estimation for lithium-ion batteries from electrochemical models. Other interests include: optimization, statistics, inverse problems for PDEs, and mathematical finance.
